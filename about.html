Get to Know Me.

+

I’ve always been drawn to figuring things out—especially when they’re messy, hands-on, and worth the effort. I like building things people can actually use. Tools that feel intuitive, that solve real problems without making more of them.

My happy place is where design, hardware, and software meet community:

  • I model with SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and OnShape and focus on designs that are cost-efficient, intuitive, and functional.
  • I craft interfaces in Figma, JavaScript, and Tailwind. The kind that feel effortless to use and stick in your mind long after.
  • I build backends with Node.js, Python, and SQLite to keep things fast and reliable.
  • I’m a firm believer in late-night snack runs with peers. Sometimes breakthroughs happen between bites and bad jokes.

I don’t think there’s ever one right answer. That’s why I keep learning, experimenting, and adjusting. In the end, I’m driven by one thing: building systems, products, and communities that help people live a little easier, work a little better, and feel a bit more connected.
